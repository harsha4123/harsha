Boruto: Naruto Next Generations is a TV series that started in 2017 and is still running. It is a sequel and spin-off of Masashi Kishimoto's Naruto. The series follows Boruto Uzumaki, the son of Naruto Uzumaki, as he and his friends try to become great ninjas. Boruto's goal is to stand out in the ninja world and leave his father's shadow behind.
